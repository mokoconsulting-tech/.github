## 🎯 What We Do

Moko Consulting is a creative and technology consultancy that helps nonprofits, small businesses,
artists, and visionaries build bold brands, powerful platforms, and thriving communities. Rooted in
open-source technology, we deliver enterprise-grade solutions that combine creativity, strategy, and
technical excellence.

### Core Services

- **ERP/CRM & NPO SaaS** - MokoCRM: business CRM, full ERP, and nonprofit editions built on Dolibarr
- **Website & Platform Development** - MokoWaaS: enterprise Joomla solutions with custom extensions
- **Google Workspace Management** - Admin, security, automation, and end-user support
- **Digital Transformation** - Modernization, integration, and process automation
- **Cloud & DevOps** - CI/CD pipelines, automation, and infrastructure
- **Preferred Partner Program** - Structured, long-term strategic collaboration frameworks

### Open Source

We actively build, use, and contribute to the open-source platforms we deploy — Dolibarr ERP/CRM and
Joomla CMS. This means our clients benefit from deeper knowledge, better customization, faster
support, and real accountability — without vendor lock-in.
